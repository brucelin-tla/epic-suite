### Dan Martell — Buyback Principle / automation frameworks
- Core rule: **"Don't hire to grow your business. Hire to buy back your time"** — invest to delegate low-value tasks so founders can focus on the few things only they can do. ([Shortform](https://www.shortform.com/summary/buy-back-your-time-vb13747-e-summary-dan-martell))
- The **Buyback Loop**: Audit (time/energy audit, separate draining tasks from fueling ones) → Transfer (offload the low-value ones) → Fill (refill the freed time with high-value work). It's meant to repeat — "what should I stop doing next?" ([Dorin Blaga](https://dorinblaga.com/the-buy-back-loop/), [Donna Pro](https://donnapro.com/delegation-leadership/buy-back-your-time/))
- The **DRIP Matrix** audits tasks by financial value vs. energy drain to decide Delegate / Replace / Invest / Produce. The **Buyback Rate** is the hourly rate below which a task should be outsourced. ([Plannerster](https://www.plannerster.com/book-guides/buy-back-your-time-summary))
- On AI specifically: **fix the process before automating it** — "automating a broken process just makes it fail faster" — and keep humans on judgment/relationship/complex-issue work; AI should make the team better, not replace it. Managing people is explicitly called out as something AI won't replicate. ([danmartell.com](https://www.danmartell.com/popular-ai-advice-you-should-never-follow-and-what-to-do-instead/), [Shortform podcast summary](https://www.shortform.com/podcast/episode/growth-stacking-show-with-dan-martell-2026-02-19-episode-summary-how-to-actually-use-ai-in-2026))

### Alex Hormozi — offers, leads, sales
- **Value Equation** ($100M Offers): Value = (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood of Achievement) ÷ (Time Delay × Effort & Sacrifice). Numerator factors create value; denominator factors destroy it — and because it's a ratio, shrinking the denominator toward zero increases perceived value fastest. ([creatoreconomy.so](https://creatoreconomy.so/p/the-value-equation-for-irresistible-products))
- **Core Four** ($100M Leads): the only four lead-gen channels are Warm Outreach, Post Free Content, Cold Outreach, and Paid Ads (warm/cold × one-to-one/one-to-many). Recommended sequencing: start with Warm Outreach + Content, add Cold Outreach and Paid Ads once the offer/sales process is proven. Rule of 100: 100 outreach contacts/day, or 100 minutes of content/day, or $100 of ads/day. ([Think 7 Figures](https://think7figures.com/alex-hormozi-100m-leads-framework/), [Greg Faxon](https://www.gregfaxon.com/blog/100m-leads-summary))
- **CLOSER framework** for the call itself: Clarify why they're here → Label the problem → expand pain → Show the "vacation" (the after-state) → handle concErns → Reinforce the decision. Objection handling uses **AAA**: Acknowledge → Associate (a past client with the same objection who got results) → Ask (for the sale again). Hormozi is explicit: being caught off guard by a common objection means you're underprepared — know your objections cold. ([Sales Coach Pro](https://www.salescoachpro.io/blog/hormozi-closer-framework), [The Follow Up](https://www.jointhefollowup.com/p/alex-hormozis-closer-sales-framework))
- **Speed to lead**: contacting a lead within 60 seconds is associated with a **391% increase** in close likelihood (Hormozi's cited figure); separately, HBR-cited research found a 400% odds drop moving from a 5- to 10-minute response window, and industry data shows the average firm takes 42 hours to respond, with 51% of leads never contacted at all and 71% of internet leads wasted on poor follow-up. ([Verse.ai](https://verse.ai/blog/speed-to-lead-statistics), [Kixie](https://www.kixie.com/sales-blog/speed-to-lead-response-time-statistics-that-drive-conversions/))

## Applying both to EPIC Suite's actual state

- The Live cockpit already builds toward Hormozi's CLOSER/AAA structure (`OBJ_BANK` objection bank, AI next-line coach) — the mechanism is sound, but it's **inert on real calls right now** because of the API-key friction. That is the single highest-leverage fix available: it's the one change that turns an already-built asset into something usable on today's calls, which is exactly the kind of high-ROI "buy back the founder's time" move (removing a manual, error-prone step from the actual revenue path) rather than new tool-building.
- Setter's silent fallback to 2 fake demo leads when the Sales Hub sync breaks is a direct collision with the 391%/60-second speed-to-lead statistic: real leads may be sitting untouched precisely while a BDR unknowingly works fake ones with no strong warning. This is a retention-and-revenue-relevant reliability gap, not a feature gap.
- Success (CS)'s lack of persistence works against Bruce's stated #2 priority (retention/education) — Martell's Buyback Loop logic (audit → transfer low-value tasks) argues checklist tracking is exactly the kind of low-judgment, high-volume task that should be safely automated/persisted so CS reps spend their time on the member relationship, not re-entering lost progress.
- Referral, per the Core Four, is functioning as EPIC's "warm outreach" channel and is already the most backend-complete tool — it needs the smallest amount of further investment (just the "Contacted" stage instrumentation) to be fully honest end-to-end.
- Admin's mixed real/fake dashboard risks Bruce (or a report to Bruce) reading fabricated funnel/leaderboard numbers as real — a Martell-style "fix the process before automating/reporting on it" violation: shipping a dashboard before the metric pipeline is real invites decisions on bad data.

## Concrete next steps for EPIC Suite (ranked by fastest path to more/better real calls closing today)

1. **Remove the Anthropic-API-key friction from the Live cockpit.** Move the AI co-pilot call behind a server-side Edge Function with a provisioned team key (mirroring the existing `fathom-webhook`/`payment-status` Edge Function pattern already in the repo) instead of a per-rep `prompt()` + `localStorage` key. This is the one fix that makes the already-built CLOSER-aligned AI coach actually usable on real calls starting tomorrow.
2. **Make the Setter/Live demo-data fallback loud, not silent.** Replace the toast with a persistent, impossible-to-miss banner ("⚠️ Not showing your real queue — contact admin") whenever `prospects`/leads fail to load, so a BDR never unknowingly burns the 60-second response window on fake leads while real ones go cold.
3. **Wire Success (CS) to real persistence.** The Supabase tables and pattern already exist elsewhere (Setter/Referral/Live) — extend the same read/write pattern to `success/index.html`'s member list and checklist state so onboarding progress survives a refresh. Directly serves the retention priority.
4. **Visually quarantine Admin's fake metrics.** Move `METRICS`/`FUNNEL`/`BOARDS`/etc. below a hard visual divider labeled clearly as illustrative-only (not a small badge) until they're wired to real queries, so no one mistakes them for real performance data.
5. **Instrument Referral's "Contacted" stage** so the funnel status is fully honest end-to-end (smallest lift of the five, since Referral is already the most complete tool).
