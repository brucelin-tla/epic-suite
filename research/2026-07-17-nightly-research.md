# Nightly Research — 2026-07-17

## Tonight's focus
Dan Martell's automation/delegation frameworks and Alex Hormozi's sales/offer/lead-gen frameworks, applied to EPIC Suite's actual current codebase state (first run — no prior reports exist yet).

## Key findings

### EPIC Suite codebase — what's actually built (audited directly, not assumed)
- There **is** a real backend: Supabase (`profiles.role`-gated auth), real Postgres tables, and real Edge Functions for Calendly booking, Stripe payment confirmation, Fathom call-recording webhooks, and commission splits. This is not a pure mockup suite.
- **Referral Partner** (`referral/index.html`): real and functional — live Supabase-backed status tracker and real commission tracking ($100/joined referral). Gap: the "Contacted" status never lights up (waiting on setter contact-event tracking), so partners can't see if a referral stalled.
- **Setter/BDR** (`setter/index.html`): real and functional — live Supabase lead queue, scripted BANT qualification, real Calendly booking onto a closer's actual calendar. No real dialer — the "call" happens on the rep's own phone; the tool is a script/logging layer around it, with no way to verify a call actually connected.
- **Closer** (`live/index.html`, 179KB, the most-developed app): real hybrid, but the "AI calling co-pilot" billed on `choose.html` is largely aspirational for real calls. The AI features (`closerCoach()`, `askRenee()`) call `api.anthropic.com` directly, but only work if a rep has manually pasted their own personal Anthropic API key via a `prompt()` — there's no app-provided key. Real Web Speech API transcription exists but only feeds the practice/demo chat, not a live phone call; the 7 pre-recorded MP3s in `live/audio/` are for one fixed demo persona, not the real product. Real telephony (Twilio) is commented "not yet built." The commission-closing Edge Function only fires for referral-sourced deals — logging "Won" on other queue items does nothing.
- **Client Success** (`success/index.html`): real auth, but the entire member workflow is fake — `MEMBERS` is a hardcoded array of 4 fictional names, checklist state lives only in memory, nothing persists across a page refresh.
- **Admin** (`admin/index.html`): real for ops plumbing (payouts, team management, Fathom sync) but the headline dashboard/funnel/leaderboard metrics are hardcoded JS arrays, explicitly labeled "Demo data" in the code.
- **Epic Life Ledger** (`ledger/index.html`, 386KB): confirmed owner-only via a hardcoded `TOOL_RANK=5` gate — Setters, Closers, and CS reps are explicitly locked out. It's a financial-illustration/retirement-modeling tool for Bruce's own consultative meetings, unrelated to running sales calls, and it has absorbed by far the most recent engineering effort of anything in the repo (dozens of CHANGELOG entries, versions b30–b45).

### Dan Martell — Buyback Principle & delegation frameworks
- Core stance: "We don't hire people to grow our business — we hire people to buy back time out of our calendar" — use money to reclaim time, not time to save money. ([Entrepreneur.com](https://www.entrepreneur.com/living/how-much-should-pay-other-people-to-take-work-off-your/443766))
- **Buyback Rate**: (annual income ÷ 2,000 hours) ÷ 4 = the max hourly rate worth paying someone/something to take a task off your plate, targeting 4x ROI on delegated time. ([Rise With Drew](https://risewithdrew.com/how-to-calculate-your-buyback-rate-dan-martells-formula-for-entrepreneurial-freedom/))
- **DRIP Matrix** (money × energy): the "Replacement" quadrant — high revenue impact, low personal energy (data entry, follow-up sequencing, meeting booking) — is what he flags as the automation priority, ahead of low-value admin. ([Rise With Drew](https://risewithdrew.com/the-four-quadrants-of-time-how-to-maximize-energy-and-income-with-dan-martells-buyback-matrix/))
- **"Closer Agent" pattern**: automate lead scraping, qualifying, enrichment, and pre-call prep summaries; keep the human for "high-value conversations" — i.e., automate the setup, not the close. ([danmartell.com](https://www.danmartell.com/agentic-ai-tools/))
- Directly relevant warning for this founder: "Some founders build first and sell later. I believe you should sell first, then build. No sense in spending time on something people haven't paid for." ([X/Dan Martell](https://x.com/danmartell/status/2044800512134951338))

### Alex Hormozi — sales, offer, lead-gen, retention frameworks
- **Value Equation**: Value = (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood of Achievement) / (Time Delay × Effort & Sacrifice). ([Greg Faxon summary of $100M Offers](https://www.gregfaxon.com/blog/100m-offers-summary))
- **Core Four** lead-gen methods ($100M Leads): warm outreach, posting free content, cold outreach, paid ads — crossing audience temperature with 1:1 vs. 1:many reach. Referral/affiliate growth is explicitly framed as the highest-leverage warm channel. ([Greg Faxon summary](https://www.gregfaxon.com/blog/100m-leads-summary), [Shortform](https://www.shortform.com/blog/100m-leads-alex-hormozi/))
- **CLOSER** call framework: Clarify → Label → Overview → Sell (the outcome, not the mechanism) → Explain away objections → Reinforce the decision post-purchase. ([Hormozi's own thread](https://x.com/AlexHormozi/status/1537193249453985792), corroborated by [Accelerator University](https://www.skool.com/acceleratoruniversity/what-is-alex-hormozis-closer-framework))
- **Funnel he tracks**: Show Rate → Offer Rate → Close Rate, with a real case study of doubling sales in 60 days after diagnosing a below-benchmark show rate (49% vs. ~70% target) as the binding constraint. ([hormozi.blog](https://hormozi.blog/3-ways-we-improved-show-rate-on-sales-calls/), [Scalelist](https://scalelist.com/cold-calling-kpis-to-track/))
- Dashboard philosophy: one dashboard, fewest/most important numbers, judged by how many people check it daily. ([sqlpatterns.com](https://sqlpatterns.com/p/how-analytics-can-make-a-massive))
- LTV:CAC target ~6–9:1 for human-touch, call-heavy sales models (vs. ~3:1 for no-touch). ([Skool/$100M Money Models notes](https://www.skool.com/100mmastery-5227/alex-hormozi-business-owners-need-to-know-this-video-notes))
- Retention: an "activation point" (engineering the member's first genuine win) and a cancel-flow value-recap intervention are called out as key churn levers. ([Accelerator University churn checklist](https://www.skool.com/acceleratoruniversity/churn-checklist-alex-hormozis-14-strategies?p=5c42090b) — community-sourced, lower confidence than the book-sourced claims above)

## Concrete next steps for EPIC Suite (ranked by what most directly speeds up closing real calls TODAY)

1. **Ship a CLOSER-stage checklist inside `live/index.html`.** Cheap, no backend/AI/telephony work — a live-call structure aid (Clarify/Label/Overview/Sell/Explain/Reinforce) that directly improves every real call run today, independent of the unfinished AI-audio feature.
2. **Fix the trust gap on "Won."** The commission Edge Function silently no-ops for non-referral deals logged as Won. A closer who logs a real win and sees no payout reflected will stop trusting (and using) the tool on real calls — this is an adoption risk, not a cosmetic one. At minimum, surface honestly when a logged deal won't trigger commission.
3. **Wire the Referral "Contacted" status.** Small, already-scoped gap; serves the warm-referral channel Hormozi's Core Four and case data both flag as highest-converting — worth fixing before any new-channel work.
4. **Replace Admin's hardcoded "Demo data" dashboard with a real Show Rate → Offer Rate → Close Rate funnel** pulled from the Supabase data that already exists (bookings, deals). This is the single dashboard Hormozi's framework calls for, and it's the prerequisite for knowing, with real numbers, where today's actual bottleneck is — right now leadership is prioritizing on hardcoded arrays.
5. **Redirect any further "AI co-pilot" engineering toward automated pre-call prep** (Martell's Closer Agent pattern — auto-surface prospect history/notes before the call starts) instead of live audio transcription + Twilio telephony. The latter is a multi-week build; validate that reps actually use the existing typed-note AI feature before investing further there.
6. **After 1–5: fix Client Success persistence** (hardcoded `MEMBERS` array, nothing saves). Real retention lever per Hormozi's activation-point/cancel-flow guidance, but it's priority #2 (retention) behind priority #1 (closing), so it's sequenced last here.

## Scope-creep flags

- **Epic Life Ledger is the clearest scope-creep risk in the whole suite.** It's owner-only, has zero sales/CRM/dialer code, and has absorbed more recent engineering effort (dozens of CHANGELOG entries, 386KB) than the entire sales-team-facing suite combined — while Setter, Closer, and Client Success all have real, load-bearing gaps still open. This matches Bruce's known pattern exactly. Recommend pausing further Ledger work until the priority-1 gaps above are closed, per Martell's "sell first, then build."
- **Building out live-call AI audio transcription + real Twilio telephony** is the natural-looking "next big feature" but is a large build with unvalidated demand — the current typed-note AI assist may not even be in active use by reps. Confirm usage before committing engineering time here; this is exactly the kind of impressive-looking tool-building that doesn't provably move the close-rate needle.
- **Expanding the Admin dashboard/leaderboard beyond the core Show/Offer/Close funnel** should be resisted — Hormozi's own guidance is fewer metrics tracked daily, not more, and a broader dashboard rebuild risks becoming its own multi-week distraction from item 4 above.
