## Key findings

### What's actually built (grounded in a full read of the repo, not assumption)
There is no backend code in this repo — every tool (`referral/`, `setter/`, `live/`, `success/`, `admin/`) is static HTML/JS calling Supabase directly; all server logic lives in named Supabase Edge Functions managed outside this repo. The lead→book→close→pay spine is genuinely wired for referral-sourced deals: real Supabase magic-link auth with rank-gated tool access (`index.html:198-273`), real referral submission and Calendly booking (`referral/index.html:360-412`), a real Setter queue reading `prospects` with real call-log/follow-up writes (`setter/index.html:295-433`), and a real Closer queue with real Calendly reschedule/cancel and real Stripe payment links + webhook-fed paid status (`live/index.html:1013-1803`). CHANGELOG v1.28.0 (2026-07-04) is the inflection point where demo data was replaced with real Sales Hub leads across the suite, and v1.48.0 (current head) made warm-referral 🏆 Won realize real commissions via `close-deal`.

Two things are still faked in ways that matter:
1. **Setter's "AI-ranked queue" isn't AI-ranked** — it sorts by raw CRM entry date, a documented workaround (`setter/index.html:611-613`).
2. **Closer's flagship feature — a live AI call copilot with transcript, BANT autofill, and objection rebuttals — only exists in a fixed, single-prospect scripted demo** (`live/index.html:1264-1326`). A real prospect instead gets `runRealCall()` (`live/index.html:1169-1180`): a bare connect timer, no transcript, no BANT autofill, and — notably — **no consent-chip UI at all**, since `showConsentChip()` only fires in the demo path. The "Verify booking → release $100" panel on real calls is also decorative: hardcoded name, no Supabase write, just a local toast (`live/index.html:484-491, 2284-2289`).

### Dan Martell — Buyback Principle / automation frameworks
The Buyback Principle: pay to delegate low-value, energy-draining tasks so time goes to your highest-value zone; his "Replacement Ladder" sequences delegation admin → delivery → marketing → sales as a business grows ([Rise With Drew](https://risewithdrew.com/dan-martells-replacement-ladder-how-to-buy-back-my-time-so-i-can-focus-on-what-energizes-me/), [ReadingGraphics](https://readingraphics.com/book-summary-buy-back-your-time/)). His 2025-2026 AI guidance is explicit that founders should automate wherever the *business itself is bottlenecked* — not whatever's trending — with lead qualification named as the highest-value automation target once a company crosses ~$1M, while relationship-building and judgment stay human ([danmartell.com](https://www.danmartell.com/4-ai-automations-behind-million-dollar-businesses-with-no-new-hires/), [Shortform](https://www.shortform.com/podcast/episode/growth-stacking-show-with-dan-martell-2026-02-19-episode-summary-how-to-actually-use-ai-in-2026)). Applied literally: EPIC Suite's actual bottleneck right now isn't "no AI on the call" — it's that the Setter queue isn't really prioritized and the live-call AI is imaginary. Martell's framework argues for automating the *queue-ranking/response-speed* problem first, since that's the real chokepoint, not the flashiest missing feature.

### Alex Hormozi — sales/offer/retention frameworks
- **Value Equation** (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood) / (Time Delay × Effort) — the denominator (removing friction/delay) is where most businesses under-invest ([SuperSummary](https://www.supersummary.com/100m-offers/summary/), [creatoreconomy.so](https://creatoreconomy.so/p/the-value-equation-for-irresistible-products)).
- **CLOSER framework** (Clarify, Label, Overview pain, Sell the vacation, Explain away concerns, Reinforce) is a 6-step call structure ([Sales Coach Pro](https://www.salescoachpro.io/blog/hormozi-closer-framework), [Hormozi Wiki](https://alexhormozi.wiki/frameworks/closer-framework-explained)) — notably, the app's own scripted demo already models this by name per CHANGELOG v1.16.0 ("CLOSER script, objection rebuttals"). The methodology is already baked in; it just isn't live.
- **Core Four lead gen** (warm outreach, free content, paid ads, cold outreach) and the **Rule of 100** ([think7figures.com](https://think7figures.com/alex-hormozi-100m-leads-framework/)) — not directly an EPIC Suite gap tonight, flagged for a future lead-source-mix review.
- **Retention**: revenue retention is the top health metric; churn is front-loaded (>20% in the first 30 days, ~10% by month 3, ~2%/mo after month 6); regular check-ins every 14-21 days and mandatory exit interviews reduce it; 80% annual retention is the B2B benchmark ([blockchain.news](https://blockchain.news/ainews/ai-powered-customer-retention-ascension-model-increase-ltv-and-reduce-cac-with-hormozi-s-strategies)). Client Success (`success/index.html`) is 100% hardcoded mock data today — no real check-in cadence is tracked anywhere.

### The connecting finding: speed-to-lead
Responding to a new lead within 5 minutes makes contact ~100x more likely and qualification ~21x more likely versus later response, per the MIT/InsideSales Lead Response Management studies cited across multiple 2026 summaries; lead quality drops ~80% after 5 minutes, and average industry response time is ~42 hours ([caseyresponse.com](https://caseyresponse.com/blog/lead-response-time-statistics), [voiso.com](https://voiso.com/articles/lead-response-time-metrics/)). This is the single most provable, cheapest lever available and it directly targets the mislabeled "AI-ranked" queue — sorting cleverness matters far less than alerting a human the instant a lead lands.

### Buy vs. build: Fathom already does more than it's wired for
Fathom (already integrated for post-call backfill, `admin/index.html:242-249,356-382`) sells a **real-time in-call coaching feature** — live talk-to-listen ratio and monologue nudges — as a paid tier, distinct from its after-the-fact transcript/summary product ([fahimai.com](https://www.fahimai.com/fathom-ai-note-taker), [pitchcraft.agency](https://pitchcraft.agency/tools/fathom/)). EPIC Suite currently only uses Fathom's after-call ingestion. Building a custom live-transcript/BANT-autofill/objection-rebuttal engine from scratch — which is what the demo's UI currently implies exists for real calls — would be a large, multi-week build. Checking whether TLA's existing Fathom plan already includes real-time coaching, and surfacing that signal inside the live cockpit, is a "buy" answer to what looks like a "build" problem.

## Concrete next steps for EPIC Suite (ranked by fastest path to better real calls today)

1. **Add the consent chip to `runRealCall()`.** It currently only fires in the scripted demo (`live/index.html`, `showConsentChip()`). This is a compliance gap on every real call happening right now, not a nice-to-have — small, fast fix, protects the business's ability to keep making real calls at all.
2. **Relabel or fix the Setter "AI-ranked" queue.** Either be honest in the UI that it's sorted by entry date, or — better and higher-leverage — replace it with a real-time new-lead alert (push/SMS/Slack ping) the instant a `prospects` row lands, so a human responds inside the 5-minute window. This is the single highest-ROI change per the speed-to-lead data above, and it's cheap.
3. **Investigate Fathom's real-time coaching tier before building anything custom for live-call AI.** If TLA's plan supports it, wire the existing signal into `live/index.html`'s real-call view. If Bruce decides a custom live-AI-copilot is still wanted as a differentiator, that's a deliberate strategic bet — make it explicit, not a default.
4. **Fix or remove the decorative "Verify booking → release $100" panel** (`live/index.html:484-491`) — it currently implies a commission action that never happens; a rep who trusts it once and gets burned will stop trusting the tool generally.
5. **Confirm with Bruce whether closer-sourced (non-referral) wins need the same `close-deal` commission wiring** that warm-referral deals got in v1.48.0 — worth a quick confirmation so reps closing non-referral deals aren't working un-paid in the tool's eyes.

Client Success (`success/`) and Admin's cosmetic metrics/leaderboards are real gaps but are correctly *not* on this list — they don't speed up closing calls today, and Bruce's stated priority order puts them below making real calls happen.
