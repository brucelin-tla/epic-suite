## Key findings (frameworks)

**Dan Martell — automation/delegation:**
- The **Buyback Principle**: use money to buy back time; the **Buyback Loop** is Audit → Transfer → Fill — identify low-value/energy-draining tasks, offload them, refill the time with revenue-driving work. ([donnapro.com](https://donnapro.com/delegation-leadership/buy-back-your-time/))
- The **DRIP Matrix** categorizes tasks by financial value vs. energy drain to decide what to Delegate/Replace/Invest/Produce; the **Replacement Ladder** is a hiring/delegation sequence (Executive Assistant → Delivery → Marketing → Sales → Leadership). ([hireinsouth.com](https://www.hireinsouth.com/post/buy-back-your-time-a-book-summary-of-dan-martells-take-on-reclaiming-freedom), [risewithdrew.com](https://risewithdrew.com/dan-martells-replacement-ladder-how-to-buy-back-my-time-so-i-can-focus-on-what-energizes-me/))
- Martell's rule of thumb for **what to automate first**: high-frequency, high-pain tasks — e.g., AI email triage cutting a 30-min/day task to ~3 minutes. ([marketingagent.blog](https://marketingagent.blog/2026/04/13/tutorial-dan-martells-buyback-system-for-time-freedom/))
- His concrete AI-CEO-Challenge sales example: an **AI voice/chat agent pre-qualifies leads with qualifying questions before they're allowed to book a meeting** (one cited case reclaimed ~95% of time previously spent on unqualified calls), plus a **5-touchpoint follow-up cadence over 21 days**. ([shortform.com](https://www.shortform.com/podcast/episode/growth-stacking-show-with-dan-martell-2025-10-10-episode-summary-how-to-get-more-customers-with-ai)) The pattern across his material: **automate qualification, scheduling, and follow-up; the actual relationship-building/selling conversation stays human** — searches for an explicit "don't automate closing" quote didn't surface a clean citation, so treat that inference as directional, not a sourced Martell quote.

**Alex Hormozi — offers, sales, retention:**
- **Value Equation** (`$100M Offers`): Value = (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood of Achievement) / (Time Delay × Effort & Sacrifice) — maximize the top, minimize the bottom; because it's a division, shrinking the denominator toward zero is where most of the leverage is. ([supersummary.com](https://www.supersummary.com/100m-offers/summary/), [creatoreconomy.so](https://creatoreconomy.so/p/the-value-equation-for-irresistible-products))
- **CLOSER framework** (`$100M Leads`/sales training): Clarify → Label → Overview (the pain cycle) → Sell the Vacation (their dream outcome, in their own words) → Explain Away Concerns (reframe objections, don't just rebut) → Reinforce the decision. Objection technique: **AAA — Acknowledge, Associate, Ask**; a price objection usually means value wasn't communicated. ([skool.com](https://www.skool.com/acceleratoruniversity/what-is-alex-hormozis-closer-framework), [jointhefollowup.com](https://www.jointhefollowup.com/p/alex-hormozis-closer-sales-framework))
- **Core Four** lead-gen channels: Warm Outreach, Content, Cold Outreach, Paid Ads — his recommended sequencing is **start with Warm Outreach + Content, add Cold Outreach, then Paid Ads once the offer/sales process is proven**. Warm outreach process: gather contacts → pick a platform → personalize → ~100 outreaches/day → refine with **ACA (Acknowledge, Compliment, Ask)**. ([shortform.com](https://www.shortform.com/blog/100m-leads-alex-hormozi/), [gregfaxon.com](https://www.gregfaxon.com/blog/100m-leads-summary))
- **Retention**: target well under ~3% monthly churn; his "Fast Cash Play" — pushing every client to a tangible win in their first 30 days — reportedly took one business's churn from 8% to 3% in six months. ([blockchain.news](https://blockchain.news/ainews/ai-powered-customer-retention-ascension-model-increase-ltv-and-reduce-cac-with-hormozi-s-strategies), [scribd.com](https://www.scribd.com/document/927850251/100m-Money-Models-Delivery-System-Retention))

## Applying both to EPIC Suite's actual state

- **TLA's compliance line matters here**: Hormozi's Value Equation numerator ("Dream Outcome," "Perceived Likelihood of Achievement") is written for outcome-guaranteeing sales contexts. TLA is education, not individualized advice — so the numerator can't be leaned on the way Hormozi teaches it (no "you will achieve X"). The real, compliant leverage is entirely in the **denominator**: cut Time Delay and Effort in the buying/onboarding process itself. That's exactly what real Stripe links + real Calendly booking in `beta/live` already do — this is the right instinct, it's just not finished (see gap below).
- **The single biggest gap between "built" and "fully functional for real sales calls"**: `beta/live`'s missing write-back. A rep can already pull a real lead, run a real call, and take a real Stripe payment — but the outcome, BANT, and notes disappear. That breaks Hormozi's CLOSER "Reinforce" + handoff step (nothing to hand Client Success), breaks any real commission calculation (which is presumably why leaderboards are still stubbed "demo"), and breaks Martell's Buyback logic in reverse — reps are manually re-doing/re-remembering work the system should retain.
- **Root `live/` being labeled "🟢 Live" on the hub while it's 100% fake is a display-truth risk**, the same category of bug this org's other codebase (Entrepreneur Paradise) explicitly guards against ("does every label the player sees match the mechanics exactly?"). A rep or Bruce clicking "Live" today gets a demo, not the real tool.
- **Client Success has no real member feed**, so Hormozi's Fast-Cash-Play/30-day-win retention mechanic — the highest-leverage lever for a membership business's LTV — literally cannot be run against real members yet. This directly serves TLA's business model (membership retention), not just the Closer path.
- **Referral Partner is the most complete, most real tool in the suite** — and it's also exactly the channel Hormozi says to start with (Warm Outreach). That's a good sign the highest-leverage existing asset and the recommended-first channel are the same tool; it argues for pushing referral-partner activation/volume over building new lead-gen surface area.
- **The AI co-pilot (`closerCoach`) already matches the Martell pattern of "AI assists, human still closes"** — it suggests the next line rather than replacing the closer. Its rough edge: in the root build it requires the rep's personal Anthropic key in `localStorage`, which is a bootstrapping/access-control gap, not a strategy gap.

## Concrete next steps for EPIC Suite (ranked by how directly they speed up closing real calls TODAY)

1. **Wire disposition/outcome/BANT/notes write-back to Supabase in `beta/live` (Closer) and `beta/setter`.** This is the one gap blocking real, ongoing pipeline use — without it "real mode" still can't sustain an actual book of business past a single call. Highest leverage, smallest, most urgent fix.
2. **Point the team at `beta/`, not root `live/`, for actual calls today — or relabel the root hub card** so nobody mistakes the fully-simulated build for the real one. Zero new code; a config/communication fix that can happen immediately.
3. **Feed Client Success a real member data source**, mirroring the `prospects`-table pattern already built for Setter/Closer, so the 30-day Fast-Cash-Play retention mechanic can run against real members. Directly serves membership retention, which is half of TLA's business model.
4. **Structure the Closer AI co-pilot's prompts around the CLOSER framework** (Clarify → Label → Overview → Sell the Vacation → Explain Away Concerns → Reinforce) instead of freeform next-line suggestions — the scaffolding (`closerCoach`) already exists, this is a prompt/logic change, not new infrastructure. Do this *after* #1–#3.
5. **Relabel every remaining "(demo)"/mock commission and leaderboard number honestly** (matching the disclaimer Admin already has) until real ledger wiring exists. Quick, compliance-and-trust-motivated copy fix, not a build.
6. **Lean into Referral Partner adoption/volume as the primary lead channel** before investing in new cold-outreach or paid-ads tooling — it's both Hormozi's recommended first channel and the most functionally complete tool already built.
