## Key findings (frameworks, cited)

**Dan Martell — Buyback Principle / Replacement Ladder** ([danmartell.com](https://www.danmartell.com/buy-back-your-time-boost-profits/), [risewithdrew.com](https://risewithdrew.com/dan-martells-replacement-ladder-how-to-buy-back-my-time-so-i-can-focus-on-what-energizes-me/)): his delegation order is **Admin → Delivery → Marketing → Sales → Leadership**, climbed rung by rung. Sales is delegated/automated *last*, not first — the framework argues a founder should keep doing sales personally longer while automating admin and delivery first. His Buyback Rate math (annual income ÷ 2,000 hours ÷ 4 = the price below which a task should be delegated) and DRIP Matrix (Delegation/Replacement/Investment/Production) are the diagnostic tools for deciding what to hand off ([readingraphics.com](https://readingraphics.com/book-summary-buy-back-your-time/), [donnapro.com](https://donnapro.com/delegation-leadership/buy-back-your-time/)).

**Martell — speed-to-lead automation, the one concretely provable ROI case**: SaaS Academy's own reported case study shows adding SMS automation for instant lead engagement cut no-show rates by ~20% ([salesmessage.com](https://www.salesmessage.com/blog/saas-academy-case-study)). A 3-level AI agent maturity framework (Level 1: AI speeds up a step you still run; Level 2: you hand it a goal and it executes, you become the PM) appears in secondary summaries but could not be traced to a primary Martell source — flagged low-confidence.

**Alex Hormozi — Value Equation** ($100M Offers): Value = (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood of Achievement) ÷ (Time Delay × Effort & Sacrifice) — confirmed across multiple independent summaries ([creatoreconomy.so](https://creatoreconomy.so/p/the-value-equation-for-irresistible-products), [mobigym.lu](https://www.mobigym.lu/blog/our-blog-1/the-hormozi-value-equation-unlocking-extraordinary-business-value-18)). His stated insight: most businesses only push the numerator (bigger promises); the denominator (reducing time-to-first-result and buyer effort) is the more neglected, more available lever.

**Hormozi — C.L.O.S.E.R. call framework** (Clarify → Label → Overview the pain cycle → Sell the vacation → Explain away objections → Reinforce the decision) and the **3 A's objection reframe** (Acknowledge → Associate → Ask), corroborated across three independent sources ([Medium](https://medium.com/@facurubinn/alex-hormozis-closer-method-to-close-35-000-usd-in-sales-in-less-than-7-days-47ac2b8f06a9), [salesfit.ai](https://salesfit.ai/blog/closer-framework-vs-human-centric-selling), [Skool](https://www.skool.com/acceleratoruniversity/what-is-alex-hormozis-closer-framework)). His **Core Four** lead sources (Warm Outreach, Cold Outreach, Content, Paid Ads) map directly onto EPIC's existing Referral (warm) and Setter (cold) tools ([Shortform](https://www.shortform.com/blog/100m-leads-alex-hormozi/)). His "who do you know facing [problem], wanting [result] in [timeframe]" referral-ask script is a specific, reusable line ([nikofischer.com](https://nikofischer.com/alex-hormozi-lead-generation-blueprint)) — flagged as secondary-sourced, not from acquisition.com directly (blocked on fetch).

**Flagged unverifiable claims** — not used in recommendations below: a "30–40% close rate benchmark" attributed to Hormozi (untraceable to a primary source) and a Martell-adjacent "95% time reclaimed via AI voice agent" statistic (looks like vendor marketing copy, not Martell's own claim).

## Concrete next steps for EPIC Suite (ranked by speed to closing real calls today)

1. **Give `runRealCall` mode a static C.L.O.S.E.R.-structured cheat sheet + BANT checklist**, reusing the objection bank and BANT UI that already exist for the fake demo call. This is a content/UI wiring change (no new pipeline) that puts Hormozi's actual call structure in front of a rep on every real call, today — the single highest-leverage, lowest-risk change available.
2. **Populate `priority_score` and join warm referrals into the Setter/Closer queues.** The ranked queue currently degrades to entry-time sorting because the score isn't populated — fix this and the "ranked queue" claim on the hub page becomes true, meaning reps call the right leads first.
3. **Add instant SMS/notification the moment a new lead lands in Setter's queue** (the one Martell automation with an actual cited before/after number — ~20% fewer no-shows). Small, scoped, provable.
4. **Persist Client Success's 3B-call and onboarding checklist to Supabase.** Zero backend today means a real member handoff can silently vanish on a page refresh — this is a retention/compliance risk, not just a UX gap, so it belongs above cosmetic fixes even though CS is priority #2 overall.
5. **Replace or remove the fake leaderboard/commission panels in Setter and Admin.** Reps who spot the fake numbers will distrust the real numbers next to them — cheap fix, protects credibility of the tools that ARE real.

## Scope-creep flags (call these out explicitly)

- **Building the real Twilio → Deepgram → Claude live-transcription pipeline for EPIC Closer is the most tempting next project and the biggest scope-creep risk on the list.** It's exactly what Martell's own Replacement Ladder says to automate *last* (Sales, rung 4) — and it's weeks of infra/latency engineering that would keep Bruce writing code instead of running the concrete cheat-sheet fix (#1 above) that ships today and needs no new integration.
- **Admin's decorative metrics/funnel/leaderboard dashboards** look impressive but are static demo numbers with zero data underneath — more polish here is pure surface work with no revenue effect.
- **Further investment in the AI roleplay trainer** (already functional for training) is a nice-to-have, not a call-closing lever — deprioritize any expansion of it.
- **Referral bonus-tier/points finalization** is real but not urgent relative to actual calls; fine to batch into a normal release, not a rush item.
- General note: several of tonight's most concrete Hormozi/Martell recommendations (script structure, referral-ask phrasing, speed-to-lead SMS) are process/copy changes usable immediately by reps by hand, independent of any EPIC Suite code shipping — worth doing in parallel with, not blocked on, the dev work above.
