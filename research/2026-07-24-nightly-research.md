## Key findings

**1. EPIC Closer (`live/index.html`) already has real data — the gap is narrower than "build a sales tool from scratch."** It authenticates via real Supabase Auth + role checks, and `loadCloserQueue()` pulls real rows from `prospects`/`bookings`/`deals`, only falling back to demo data on query failure (`live/index.html:1013-1073`). Logging outcomes, bookings, and closed deals already call real Supabase Edge Functions (`close-deal`, `calendly-book`, etc.). So reps can already work real leads through this tool today.

**2. The advertised "AI listens live and feeds you lines" experience is demo-only.** On a real lead, `runRealCall()` explicitly does nothing but toast "🎙️ live — no script, track it yourself" (`live/index.html:1169-1180`), and the code says outright: *"Prototype — simulates the live-listening loop for the team demo. Real version: Twilio audio → Deepgram → Claude → this UI"* (`live/index.html:568`). No telephony code (Twilio, Deepgram, WebSocket call audio) exists anywhere in the repo. Today, a rep dials through an outside phone system and uses this page as a manual BANT/notes/outcome logger.

**3. There is a real, working AI feature on real calls — but it's built the wrong way.** `closerCoach()` sends the rep's typed notes straight to `https://api.anthropic.com/v1/messages` from the browser, using an API key each rep pastes in and that persists in `localStorage` (`live/index.html:1553-1603`). No backend proxy. This is functional today, but every rep needs their own key and it's exposed client-side — the one piece of "AI helps on a real call" infrastructure that half-exists is also the one with a real security/ops gap.

**4. Dan Martell's own portfolio pattern for AI + sales is "support the human, don't replace them."** Research into his companies' AI programs shows they're "layered into messaging and sales enablement tools that support human conversations at scale" rather than fully automating the sales conversation itself ([Dan Martell — 4 AI Automations](https://www.danmartell.com/4-ai-automations-behind-million-dollar-businesses-with-no-new-hires/), via search synthesis). That's exactly EPIC Closer's original design intent (AI co-pilot next to a human closer) — the concept is validated by his practice, it's just underbuilt for real calls right now.

**5. Martell's Buyback Loop (Audit → Transfer → Fill) and DRIP Matrix (Delegate / Replace / Invest / Produce) give a sequencing lens, not just a "should we automate" answer.** Tasks that are low-money/low-energy get delegated or killed fast; tasks below your Buyback Rate get bought back immediately if someone/something else can do them ([Buy Back Your Time summary — Teamly](https://www.teamly.com/blog/buy-back-your-time-by-dan-martell-chapter-2/); [Master Research Dossier](https://www.thewaitersacademy.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/09/Dan-Martell-%E2%80%94-Master-Research-Dossier-v1.pdf)). Manual BANT-tagging and note-taking mid-call is a low-skill, high-frequency task — a textbook "Replace with a tool" candidate — while the full real-time audio pipeline (Twilio→Deepgram→Claude) is a large, multi-week "Produce" investment, not a quick delegate.

**6. Hormozi's CLOSER framework (Clarify, Label, Overview Pain, Sell the vacation, Explain away concerns, Reinforce) and the AAA objection pattern (Acknowledge, Associate, Ask again) are a structured six/three-step script, not a black box** ([Sales Coach Pro](https://www.salescoachpro.io/blog/hormozi-closer-framework); [The Follow Up](https://www.jointhefollowup.com/p/alex-hormozis-closer-sales-framework)). That structure can be encoded as UI prompts/checklist steps directly in `runRealCall()` today — no AI, no telephony required — and would give reps real-time coaching value that's currently missing on real calls.

**7. Hormozi's Value Equation** — Value = (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood) / (Time Delay × Effort) — says the denominator (removing delay and effort) is where most companies under-invest ([creatoreconomy.so](https://creatoreconomy.so/p/the-value-equation-for-irresistible-products); [Greg Faxon summary](https://www.gregfaxon.com/blog/100m-offers-summary)). Applied to TLA's sales calls: the Ledger tool is a "Perceived Likelihood" engine (it makes the projected outcome credible), but if it's slow, complex, or requires owner-only setup mid-call, it's adding Effort/Time Delay against the same equation it's trying to serve.

**8. Hormozi's retention research: a fast, well-defined "activation point" cuts churn hard** — one cited case took churn from 8% to 3% in 6 months by honing onboarding to a "recoup investment in 30 days" milestone, and regular 14–21 day check-ins catch problems early ([Hormozi retention playbook notes](https://github.com/claes-work/alex-hormozi-clone/blob/main/wiki/sources/2025-100m-retention-playbook.md)). Relevant to the `success/` (Client Success) app and to Entrepreneur Paradise's retention role, but out of scope for tonight's EPIC-Suite-first focus — flagged for a future night.

## Concrete next steps for EPIC Suite (ranked by what most directly speeds up closing real calls TODAY)

It may well be core sales infrastructure (it's the tool that makes the "Dream Outcome" credible in Hormozi's Value Equation) — but the volume of near-daily polish releases, versus evidence it's actually shortening real closes, hasn't been established here. Recommend Bruce confirm it's in active use on real appointments, not just being refined in isolation.
