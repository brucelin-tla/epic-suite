### Dan Martell — automation/delegation frameworks, applied
- **Buyback Principle**: "Don't hire to grow your business. Hire to buy back your time" — use money to buy time, not vice versa. [buybackyourtime.com](https://www.buybackyourtime.com/) / [Martell Method](https://martellmethod.com/buy-back-your-time)
- **DRIP Matrix** (task triage): Delegation (low-value/draining — offload first) → Replacement (high-value but draining — outsource eventually) → Investment (energizing, not yet revenue — keep, grow) → Production (high-value AND energizing — the founder's core, stays human). [abroadassistant.com](https://abroadassistant.com/blog/discover-how-drip-matrix-can-transform-your-day/)
- **Replacement Ladder**: delegate in this order — (1) Executive Assistant, (2) Delivery, (3) Marketing, (4) Sales (lead gen/conversion/follow-up), (5) Leadership. Sales is deliberately *not* first. [risewithdrew.com](https://risewithdrew.com/dan-martells-replacement-ladder-how-to-buy-back-my-time-so-i-can-focus-on-what-energizes-me/)
- **"Identify the bottleneck first, then automate it with AI"** — automate where the business specifically slows down, not by trend. [danmartell.com/agentic-ai-tools](https://www.danmartell.com/agentic-ai-tools/)
- **4 AI automations behind lean, no-new-hire businesses** he names: outbound/sales-enablement AI co-pilots, AI voice-agent lead qualification (calls a new lead right after form-fill), AI-assisted onboarding/delivery, AI-driven daily financial reporting. [danmartell.com](https://www.danmartell.com/4-ai-automations-behind-million-dollar-businesses-with-no-new-hires/)
- **What he says stays human**: closing and relationship-building. AI should handle "all the other stuff" so humans focus on empathy, persuasion, and "the celebration and connection" — framed explicitly as "a very human process." [danmartell.com](https://www.danmartell.com/how-to-get-so-many-customers-with-ai-it-feels-illegal/)
- *Caveat*: danmartell.com pages returned HTTP 403 to direct fetch; the above is triangulated across multiple independent search results describing the same primary source, not a verbatim read of the page. Treat as paraphrase-level, not direct quote.

### Alex Hormozi — sales/offer frameworks, applied
- **Value Equation** ($100M Offers): Value = (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood of Achievement) / (Time Delay × Effort & Sacrifice) — he argues most businesses over-invest in the numerator when the denominator (killing friction/wait time) is the bigger lever. [Greg Faxon summary](https://www.gregfaxon.com/blog/100m-offers-summary)
- **Core Four + Rule of 100** ($100M Leads): the only four lead channels are Warm Outreach, Cold Outreach, Free Content, Paid Ads; discipline = 100 outreach contacts/day OR 100 minutes of content/day OR $100/day ads, then "More → Better → New." [Greg Faxon summary](https://www.gregfaxon.com/blog/100m-leads-summary)
- **CLOSER framework** (his own published sales-call structure): **C**larify why they're here → **L**abel the problem → **O**verview past pains → **S**ell the vacation (pitch the transformation, not the mechanism) → **E**xplain away objections → **R**einforce the decision. Primary source, Hormozi's own post: [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/alexhormozi_the-closer-framework-c-clarify-why-they-activity-7373739944260849664-fJmw)
- **Triple A objection handling**: Acknowledge → Associate (reframe the objection as normal buying behavior, a sign of progress) → Ask for the sale again. Taught on his podcast "The Game." [Shortform summary, Ep. 810](https://www.shortform.com/podcast/episode/the-game-w-alex-hormozi-2024-12-20-episode-summary-closing-made-easy-the-triple-a-framework-explained-ep-810)
- *Explicitly could not confirm*: specific numeric follow-up cadences or churn benchmarks attributed to Hormozi only turned up in third-party aggregator posts without a traceable primary source — excluded rather than guessed.

## Concrete next steps for EPIC Suite (ranked by what most directly speeds up closing real calls TODAY)

1. **Kill the per-rep Anthropic-key setup friction on the Closer tool.** Right now `closerCoach()`/`askRenee()` only work if each individual rep manually pastes their own personal API key into `localStorage` (`live/index.html:1609-1611`); with no key, the default experience is a fully fake scripted call. Move the Anthropic call behind a shared Supabase edge function (one org-held key, same pattern already used for `payment-status`/`admin-invite`) so every rep gets real AI co-pilot support with zero setup, the first time they open the tool, on a real call, tonight. This is the single highest-leverage fix — it turns the app's flagship feature from "opt-in for the technically inclined" to "on by default for the whole team."
2. **Re-prompt `closerCoach()` around Hormozi's CLOSER stages + Triple A objections**, instead of a generic "next line" suggester. Cheap (prompt-engineering, not new infrastructure) once #1 ships the shared backend — makes the AI's suggestions structurally sound sales guidance (clarify → label → pains → sell the transformation → handle objections with Acknowledge/Associate/Ask-again → reinforce) instead of ad hoc.
3. **Wire Client Success to real data.** It's currently the only tool in the five-stage funnel with zero live Supabase connectivity — a rep cannot run a real onboarding call through it. Mirror the pattern already proven in Setter/Admin (real table reads, demo-array fallback only on error) for the member queue and both checklists.
4. **Flip the Fathom webhook secret.** `admin/index.html:362` — call-recording ingestion is coded and deployed but inert until an operator sets one signing secret; this reads as a five-minute config task, not a build task, and unblocks whatever coaching/review value the Fathom pipeline was built for.
5. **Correct or retire `DEPLOY.md`.** It documents a deploy process for a directory structure (`sales-cockpit/prototype/...`) that no longer exists — low cost, but stale process docs are exactly the kind of thing that causes a bad live push later.

## Scope-creep flags

- **Epic Life Ledger dominates recent engineering effort, and it isn't a sales-call tool.** The git log shows the last several weeks of commits are almost entirely "Ledger b29" through "Ledger b45+" (chronic-illness riders, wizard redesigns, carrier-illustration recalibration) — a deep, sophisticated, single-owner financial-modeling tool gated to the owner only, not part of the Referral → Setter → Closer → Client Success funnel. This is the clearest visible instance of the pattern flagged at the top of this brief: real engineering hours going into a well-crafted but tangential tool while the actual rep-facing blockers above (AI-key friction, Client Success having no live data) sat unaddressed. Not a call to stop Ledger work outright — but it should not out-pace items 1–4 above, which move revenue-producing calls, not modeling precision.
- **Martell's "3-Level AI Agent" / autonomous-agent-org content is not the current bottleneck.** The gap in EPIC Suite today is basic data wiring and setup friction (a rep pasting an API key), not insufficient agent sophistication. Building a more elaborate multi-agent orchestration layer for the Closer tool right now would be solving a problem the app doesn't have yet, at the expense of the problem it does have.
- **A fully autonomous AI closer (vs. a co-pilot) is a redline, not just scope creep.** Martell's framework explicitly keeps closing/relationship-building human even as AI absorbs "everything else," and the codebase's own owner-flagged comment (`live/index.html:988-1004`) already caught the fabricated-dialogue risk of the scripted demo. Any future direction that drifts from "AI suggests, human decides and speaks" toward "AI runs the call" should be treated as a hard stop, not a feature request.
- **The hub's own "future" roadmap items** (referral leaderboard, testimonial builder, cheat sheets — `index.html:289-293`) are already correctly deprioritized in-code behind "Building now" and "Future plan" — good instinct already in place, just worth reinforcing: none of these move a call forward today and shouldn't jump the queue ahead of items 1–4.
